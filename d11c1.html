  • D11C1

The calculation of the D11C1 allows the assessment of the anthropogenic impulsive sound in water.

The Commission Decision (EU) 2017/848 define the criterion D11C1-Anthropogenic impulsive sound in water as:

The spatial distribution, temporal extent, and levels of anthropogenic impulsive sound sources do not exceed levels that adverselt affect populations of marine animals.

And set the following units of measurement for the criteria:

Number of days per quarter (or per month if appropriate) with impulsive sound; proportion (percentage) of unit areas or extent in square kilometres (km2) of assessment area with impulsive sound sources per year.

This portal provides a tool for the calculation of the spatial distribution and the temporal extent of D11C1. Visit the section "Map" and play!
